**Call date:** 2026-07-09 · **Spoke with:** managing partner · **Duration:** 20 min

## Pain points (their words)
- "Every new matter starts with someone in the office re-typing the same client
  details into three places."
- "We chase clients for documents by phone. Sometimes for weeks. Nobody owns it."
- "I don't want another portal our clients won't log into."

## Tools used today
- Intake: paper forms + the website contact form (goes to a shared inbox)
- Matter tracking: practice management software (legacy, keeps it)
- Email: Outlook; no automation of any kind

## Timeline / urgency
- Wants the document-chasing problem gone "before the fall rush" — ~8 weeks.

## Objections raised
- Skeptical of anything that emails clients automatically "sounding like a robot."
- Burned before by a consultant who "disappeared after the invoice."

## Read
Warm. Asked about price unprompted, asked "what would the first two weeks look
like." Wants a small first step, not a platform.
